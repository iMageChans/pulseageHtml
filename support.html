FAQ

How to Use PulseAge on Apple Watch?

1. Install PulseAge app on your Apple Watch

2. Open the app and allow health data access permissions

3. Sign in with your Apple ID

4. View real-time heart rate and stress data on the watch face

5. Swipe left to view detailed health metrics

6. Tap any metric to view historical trends

How Does Apple Watch Monitor Stress in Real-time?

PulseAge utilizes Apple Watch's advanced sensors to analyze your stress levels through the following metrics:

  • Heart Rate Variability (HRV)
  • Resting Heart Rate
  • Respiratory Rate
  • Activity Level
  • Sleep Quality

The system analyzes these data in real-time to provide accurate stress assessment and management recommendations.

How do I set up my PulseAge device?

Setting up your PulseAge device is simple. Download the PulseAge app from the App Store, create an account, and follow the on-screen instructions to pair your device.

What devices are compatible with PulseAge?

PulseAge is compatible with iPhone 8 and later models running iOS 14 or later. We're working on expanding compatibility to other devices.

How accurate is the heart rate monitoring?

Our heart rate monitoring technology is designed to provide accurate readings, but it's not a medical device. For medical-grade accuracy, please consult with healthcare professionals.

How do I interpret my HRV data?

HRV (Heart Rate Variability) is a measure of the variation in time between heartbeats. Higher HRV generally indicates better cardiovascular fitness and stress resilience.
